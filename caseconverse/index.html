Converse
One Star

A social campaign for the young generation in China

Switching up the game

Defining the Rules

One Star is Converses highlight line-up for the next generation of Chinese. As the brand itself is a timeless icon of youth culture around the world the pressure was high to say the least. Having to create a campaign that appeals to a new generation of young Chinese who do not appreciate to be told what to do, how to act and what to say, didnt make it any easier when essentially trying to tell them to shop OneStars. Keeping all this in mind, we couldnt run with a standard campaign but had to think outside the norm.

Our Strategy

#%$% Ratings, stay true to yourself with every step

We embraced our One Star, took it as a literal rating system and then flipped it upside down. Everyone wants to receive five stars everywhere, on every app, on every review, and so on. But for us rated One Star meant so much more. It represents the proud rejection of norms to celebrate the mentality of a young generation of visionary misfits. Changing the rules of the game not just verbally but across designs became the key narrative. We broke perfect visuals on purpose and held it up as a mark of pride. We let everyone now that they can have their five stars, as along as we could have One Star, the only one thats true to ourselves.

First off, proudly introducing the faces of One-Star

Tizzy T, _Anna, Oh Hyuk & Vinida were the faces selected for One Star we let them represent that more than loud and clear across WeChat & Weibo.

Bringing imperfection into everything

Our design and approach were centered on being true to oneself above perfect layout & texture. We didnt just want to say it but also show it across everything during the campaign.

Staying real every step of the way

Using further Weibo KOL we further pushed the narrative to show that no matter how tall, short or anything else you are, if you stay real with every step it doesnt matter what you are.

Show don't tell
